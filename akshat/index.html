TASK 2: Arrange fibers according to Optical Fiber Colour Code Scheme

Step 1: Click on colored fibers to arrange according to OFCC scheme.




TASK 3: Splicing two optical fibers end to end using Splicer.

STEP 1: a) Click Wear button to wear safety goggles.


b) Click on Cloth to cleanup table surface.


c) Click Add button to take Safety Mat on the table.




STEP 2: a) Click Add button to bring cable on the table.


b) Click on cable at given mark to cut it with desire length.


c) Use Round Cutter to make circular cut in the cable at 1m length from the end.




STEP 3: a)Use Cable Sheath Stripper to remove the jacket of the cable.


b) Click on the Open fibers to remove one fiber from it.


c) Click on the tissue paper to clean blue fiber.


d) Click on the Next button to see zoom view of blue fiber.




STEP 4: a) Click to Add button to bring Protection Sleeve on the table.


b) Click on the Protection Sleeve to place it over blue fiber.


c) Click on blue fiber to strip its outer jacket.


d) Click on lint-free tissue soaked in isopropyl alcohol to clean striped fiber.





STEP 5: a) Click on Add button to bring cleaver on the mat.


b) Click on cleaned fiber to put it on the Cleaver.


c) Click on the Fiber Holder lid to close it.


d) Click on the Cleaver upcover to close the cleaver.


e) Click on the Cleaver upcover to open the cleaver.


f) Click on the Fiber Holder Lid to open it.


g) Click on fiber to remove it from Cleaver.




STEP 6: a) Click on Add button to bring Splicer on the mat.


b) Click on Splicer to bring zoom view of splicer top.


c) Click on Splicer Wind Protector to open it.


d) Click on the Fiber Holders to open it.


e) Click on cleaved fibers for placing on the V Groove of the Splicer .


f) Click on Fiber Holders to close it.


g) Click on Splicer Wind Protector to close it.


h) Click on Splicer Screen area for zoom view of Screen.


i) Click on View key to see the cleaved fiber ends form 2 dimensions.


j) Click on Set key for generating Arc to fuse the fibers.




STEP 7: a) Click on Splicer top area to bring zoom view of splicer from top.


b) Click on Wind Protector of splicer to open it.


c) Click on both of the fiber holders.


d) Click on the joined fiber to take it out from the groove.


e) Click on the protection sleeve to slide it on joint.


f) Click on heater area on splicer to view heater zoom view.


g) Click on sleeved fiber to put it on heater.


h) Click on Heat button on splicer console to start heating process.


i) Click on the heated and sleeved wire to remove it from heater.




STEP 8: a) Click on Add button to bring Splice Tray on the table.


b) Click on the each fiber one by one to fit it in Splice Tray.


c) Our fiber has been spliced now. Thank you for performing experiment.
